the london racers was a british ice hockey club based in london england formerly members of the elite ice hockey league 
although founded in it claimed to be a successor of the harringay racers club established in 
due to a lack of suitable ice facility the club suspended its team operations part way through the season 
it was the last professional ice hockey club to play in london 
formed and organized in only a few weeks the racers played their home games at alexandra palace 
erik zachrisson captained the team 
their first season was a difficult one as the team failed to win for some games 
finally a win arrived with a victory over the cardiff devils 
another win followed as the team beat the basingstoke bison but the team finished with a ghastly record still as of the worst in league history 
the team moved to the capacity lee valley ice centre in leyton east london although the arena was considered small for elite league hockey 
the offseason saw the hiring of former american hockey league player dennis maxwell as coach who assembled a much stronger team including perennial british all star steve moria and with the nhl lockout defenceman eric cairns and centre scott nichol from the national hockey league 
they made the playoffs in the sixth slot out of seven teams with a record but were eliminated by the cardiff devils

days later on november a piece of plexiglass was smashed 
when the same section of glass smashed again at a training session the following tuesday serious questions regarding the safety of players and spectators were raised 
unable to find a new venue or secure agreement on improving safety at lee valley the racers withdrew their team from competitions on november and subsequently folded
